Reach the World

I connected with an elementary school classroom in Rockland, New York, during my research stay in Germany and provided weekly blog posts about life in a foreign country and being a young paleoclimatologist. I answered their questions about meteorology, life in a real laboratory, and cross-cultural communication.

I developed a push-in activity which I shared with students during a visit to their classroom in January, 2024. The lesson introduced students to the concept of a paleo-proxy and to evidence for historical climatic change in their own direct surroundings.
